Game Room Ideas

A game room is a custom space designed around your favorite activities. Your "game room" could take the form of an adults-only lounge for pool and poker, a kids' playroom, or a home theater the whole family can enjoy. The thing that unites all of these ideas is the desire to create a dedicated hang-out space you can enjoy with your guests (or by yourself) without disturbing other family members.

  • Install a built-in entertainment center that's ready for a TV, speakers, media players, and video game consoles.
  • Have comfortable seating, including extra that you can pull out for larger-than-normal gatherings.
  • Build plenty of shelving to keep board games and game books.
  • Install plenty of task, accent, and ambient lighting so everyone can see and to tie the room together.
  • Add a bar for drinks, snacks, and a touch of luxury.
  • Add storage for the extra things your games may need: additional controllers, decks of cards, dice, paper, pencils, etc.
  • Install soundproofing to minimize noise leaking into the rest of the house.
  • For playrooms, integrate kid-friendly elements like big windows, indoor slide or swing, a climbing wall, and chalkboard wall.

What makes a truly epic game room is a space that reflects your personality and design tastes, whether modern, traditional, or contemporary. Without a consistent theme, the room will feel bland. The room will also feel disconnected if the game room design doesn't get with the rest of your home.
