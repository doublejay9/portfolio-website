What are Tenant Improvements?

Many new commercial buildings are left as empty shells, with each rental unit consisting of four walls, a couple of doors and windows, and unfinished floors and ceilings. Without interior walls, plumbing, electrical, and other finishing work, it's not a complete building, meaning you're not ready for business. That's where remodeling companies like Aeyo Construction can help.

Tenant improvements (TI) are building alterations done to a rented space to serve the tenant's needs better. You'll often hear commercial contractors call the entire TI project a "buildout." These improvements range from installing new electrical outlets and energy-efficient lighting to a complete interior overhaul.
